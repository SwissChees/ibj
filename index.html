August 6 - Campaign Monitor Youth Fundraiser Email

Email versions

  • Version 1: This does not work for many Outlook applications because Outlook frickin' sucks.
    I used divs, but Outlook disregards margin and padding for div elements.
  • Version 2: This was my version of the flyer. I converted all the divs into tables (using Gemini).
    Then I added some more details.
  • Final: The one that we sent out on Campaign Monitor to nearly 18,000 people.

Stuff I learned

  • Outlook is the worst.
  • Gmail and Outlook don't support negative margins.
  • Getting people to read your campaign emails is difficult.
    • 17,150 emails sent.
    • Only 573, or 3%, of recipients clicked on a link.
    • Of those 573 only 2 people, or 0.3%, donated.*
      * These numbers are as of August 8, 2025.
